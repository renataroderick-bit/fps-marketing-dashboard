Key Insights for Management
What the numbers mean in plain business terms
💵
$100/lead is a good number for B2B
A recurring cleaning contract is worth $1,000–5,000/month. If we close 1 in 5 leads, each contract costs us ~$500 to acquire — a strong ROI in commercial cleaning.
📈
$500/week limits us to ~5 leads/week
Current budget generates consistent leads, but volume is capped. Increasing to $750–1,000/week could double lead flow without significantly changing cost per lead.
🏆
Floor Care & Strip-Wax: highest margin
These services have the best profit margin. Our Floor Cleaning ad group should be the top priority for optimization — higher bids, better copy, dedicated landing page.
🎯
Dunkin' + Schools: active outreach now
Franchise visits (Renée) + school direct mail launching this quarter. These are contract-first channels — one Dunkin' franchisee with 5 locations = significant recurring revenue.
